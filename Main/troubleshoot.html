Troubleshooting Guide

Games are not working correctly, or will not provide correct functionality

• I am most certainly working on it. Feel free to open an issue on GitHub if the issue hasn't been resolved.


Animations are not smooth, or inconsistent

• This is most likely due to your browser lapsing somewhere in html parsing. Try a different browser, or update.


Games are not functioning as expected

• Most games are in-developement, so any problems are to be expected. I am working on them.
